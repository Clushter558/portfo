Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and workflow

What services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of web development and design services including custom website development, responsive web design, e-commerce solutions, web application development, UI/UX design, website maintenance, and performance optimization.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex web applications can take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

What is your typical process for working with new clients?

My process typically includes: 1) Initial consultation to understand your needs, 2) Proposal and quotation, 3) Design mockups and wireframes, 4) Development and coding, 5) Testing and revisions, 6) Launch and deployment, and 7) Post-launch support and maintenance.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and functioning smoothly. This includes regular updates, security checks, performance optimization, and content updates as needed.

What is your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, I can arrange milestone-based payments. All payment terms will be clearly outlined in our project agreement.

Can you help with existing websites or only new builds?

I work with both new projects and existing websites. Whether you need a complete redesign, specific improvements, bug fixes, or performance optimization for your current site, I can help enhance and update your existing web presence.
